Who we are

Lighthall badminton club is a Solihull based club. We are a competitive but friendly club currently with excess of 35 members. We welcome any player of a decent standard. If you are looking for a suitable club, why not come along? However we are not able to take in juniors (under 16). Visits should be pre-arranged as we may not be able to accommodate visitors at all times.

When and Where

We meet on Monday nights at Langley School (Kineton Green Road , Solihull , B92 7ER.) from 7.30- 10.30p.m. Also at Lode Heath School (Lode Lane, Solihull B91 2HW) on Thursday nights from 8.00- 10.00p.m. We have four courts in both halls and we use quality feather shuttles.

Competitive Badminton

We have several teams in both the Solihull badminton league and Birmingham badminton league, and would be particularly interested in new players of competitive league standard.

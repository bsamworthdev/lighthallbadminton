Who we are

Lighthall badminton club is a Solihull based club. We are a small but friendly club with about 30 members. We welcome any players of a decent standard.We are currently accepting new members. Why not come along?

When and Where

We meet on Monday nights at Langley School from 7.30- 10.30p.m. Also at Lode Heath School on Thursday nights from 8.00- 10.00p.m. We have four courts in both halls.

Joining Us

Registration fee is £ 214.50 a year. Payment covers shuttles, court hire, affiliation the Badminton Association of England (B A of E) and Insurance. Also, you can come along as a guest for £ 5 a time for up to three weeks.

Teams

We normally have several teams in the Solihull and Birmingham leagues, and are always looking for new players. For the 2010/11 season we have registered:

  • Two Mens teams in the Birmingham League.
  • One Mixed team in the Birmingham League.
  • Two Mens teams in the Solihull League.
  • One mixed(or combination) team in the Solihull League.
